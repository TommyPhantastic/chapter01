About Us

We are a small, local diner that offers breakfast and lunch.

Our main goal here is to give every customer the best food in the area!

The owner and co-owner are shown below.

Owner

Pete started this diner with some of his families home made recipes. He set out with one goal, and that was to make people happy with his food!

He founded the diner in 2003 and has been making people smile ever since.

Co-Owner

Judy, Petes wife, is the co-owner of this wonderful diner. She is the beautiful face that greats you everytime you walk inside!
